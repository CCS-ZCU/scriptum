This most significant holiday is one to enjoy, to join a parade, to watch the fire-work display, to celebrate freedom publicly or privately. This great, beautiful land stretching from the Atlantic in the east to the Pacific in the west, from- Canada in the north to Mexico in the south, with its severe and snowy cold to subtropical climate, its extensive plains, majestic mountains, deserts, lakes, rivers, populous cities and small towns, rich farmlands producing an overabundance of food, its great industrial and technological development and its inventive and industrious people of all races, cultures, languages and religious beliefs, are the grounds for celebrating the Glorious Fourth. There is no country whose people would not wish to settle in the United States, to have the opportunities and freedoms and become a successful member of the contemporary, energetic stream of American life, where all have a chance to develop fully, to study, to participate in public affairs or keep his privacy. It seems often that an immigrant esteems America’s greatness more intensely than, many a native. lie makes preparations for citizenship with serious respect, considering it’s an honor, which native proud ciiizens sometimes fail to feel. It is sometimes forgotten that American independence and freedom were fought for and won in bloody battles and more blood was spilled to keep the country united in freedom. Then too, the United States sent her men to fight in many parts of the world to help preserve freedom and independence for others to keep them from being destroyed but at the same time, knowing well that it did so and continued to do so in its own interest to guard its own democratic institu- 1 ions. VZESTUP DOLARU A AMERIČTÍ TURISTÉ Evropský tisk v minulých dnech předpovídal, že letošní léto bude návratem amerických turistů do Evropy, kde se jich v nedávných letech objevovalo stále méně.
