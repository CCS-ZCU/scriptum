Mo sc o ne Proclamation The 68th anniversary of Czechoslovak Independence Day is being celebrated on October 30, 1976. On this date, Czechoslovak Americans celebrate the struggle for freedom that the citizens of their homeland fought for so valiantly 68 years ago. The celebration is particularly significant in this year of the American bicentennial, since the aspirations of the Czechoslovak people in 19ÍÍ8 closely paralleled those of our Founding Fathers in 1776. The American SOKOL Organization and the Czechoslovak National Council of America are celebrating Czechoslovak Independence Day in a special program on Saturday, October 30, 1976 at Sokol Hall in San Francisco. NOW, THEREFORE, I, George R. Moscone, Mayor of the City and County of San Francisco do hereby proclaim Thursday, October 28, 1976 as CZECHOSLOVAK INDEPENDENCE DAY in San Francisco and do urge all citizens to be aware of th,e struggle of the Czechoslovak people to achieve the basic freedoms of live which we enjoy in America. IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the Seal of the City and County of San Francisco to be affixed this twenty-sixth day of October nineteen hundred and seventy-six. George R.
