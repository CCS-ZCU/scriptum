Stir in eggs, raised yeast and flavoring. Sift flour on a board, add first mixture and knead into a smooth dough, that does not stick to the board. Let rise until double in bulk. Cut into even, egg-sized pieces. Press each piece into a square 3 x 3”« Put half a teaspoon of filling in the middle, draw the corners up firmly together over the filling. Put on well greased sheet or pan, 2 inches apart. Let rise again, brush with egg beaten with 1 tbs. milk. Sprinkle with crumb mixture. and bake at degrees for about 30 minutes.
