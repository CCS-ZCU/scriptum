Lautenberg, D-N.J., today announced that young men and women interested in seeking nominations to the U.S. Air Force, Merchant Marine, Military and Naval Academies must apply no later than .October 12, 1984 for the class entering in July, 1985. Applicants must be legal residents of the State of New Jersey; at least 17 years of age; but not past their 22nd birth■ day on July 1 of the year of admission;.citizens of the United States; unmarried and have no children. The SAT or ACT exams must be taken and scores reported. The SAT administered on June 2, 1984 is the last SAT which can be taken if results are to be received prior to the selection of nominees for the Academies. Applicants will be screened on the basis of their high school grades, class rank, college entrance examination scores, extra-curricular activities, leadership potential, motivation and recommendations. Candidates interested .in being considered for a nomination must submit a completed application form postmarked no later than October 12, 1984. Application packets will be mailed out in May and can be obtained by writing to Senators Bradley and Lautenberg. The deadline for receipt of all necessary support information is October 22, 1984 for Senator Bradley and November 14, 1984 for Senator Lautenberg. It is the applicant’s responsibility to submit all the required information and to keep it updated. The deadlines for receipt of information have been set to ensure adequate time to asses each candidate’s qualifications. High school principals and guidance counselors are encouraged to make students aware of the educational opportunities offered by the Academies, and to ensure that all necessary information is available to interested students. Young men and women seeking nominations should write to both their United States Senators and their Congressional Representative.
