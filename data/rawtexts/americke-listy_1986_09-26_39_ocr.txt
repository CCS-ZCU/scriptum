Last month, the Centers for Disease Control CDC) reported that the incidence of measles in the first six months of 1986 increased by 118 percent over the first half of 1985, and New Jersey is among those states showing a marked increase. Earlier this year, during budget hearings of the health and human services appropriations subcommittee on which f serve in the House, 1 asked CDC officials about our national immunization efforts, with an emphasis on ensuring that affordable vaccine for preventable diseases remain available for state and local immunization programs.
