We were gratified to hear on the eve of the election your statement con- cerning the situation in Czechoslovakia and your determination to press for a total withdrawal of the Soviet troops from that country. We as well as the people of Czechoslovakia, understand full well that the crisis in that area cannot be resolved by military action but by political means, and it is our hope that you will use your ínfluence to promote a just solution for the present situation which is unberable for European security axi intolcrable from the point of human rights as well.
