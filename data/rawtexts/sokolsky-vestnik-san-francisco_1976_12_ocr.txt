DECEMBER SEEMS TO BE THE SHORTEST MONTH OF THE YEAR, TRUE, DECEMBER DOES HAVE 31 DAYS. BUT CAN YOU REALLY CALL THEM DAYS, THOSE LITTLE SPANS THAT ZIP PAST BETWEEN THANKSGIVING AND CHRISTMAS? CERTAINLY NOT. THE THANKSGIVING TURKEY VANISHES; YOU TURN AROUND AND THERE IS THE CHRISTMAS TREE WAITING TO BE DECORATED CHRISTMAS IS TRULY FOR CHILDREN - THOSE WE HAVE - AND FOR US WHEN WE USED TO BE. THE CHRISTMAS SPIRIT COMES ON ME MORE SLOWLY THAN IT USED TO, BUT IT COMES. IT COMES FOR SURE. YOU REMEMBER: WHEN WE WERE YOUNG, CHRISTMAS WAS THE PERFECT CERTAINTY THAT THE WORLD WAS GOOD. YOU FEEL SOME SORT OF EXCITEMENT IN THE AIR AND YOU HAVE THE FEELING THAT FOR ONE BRIEF PERIOD EVERYONE IS A FRIEND. THIS SPIRIT IS ONE THAT MOST OF US CAPTURE AT CHRISTMAS. BUT WOULDN'T IT BE MARVELOUS IF WE COULD HOLD ON TO IT THROUGHOUT THE YEAR? WE SHOULD PRACTICE THE TRUTH OF THE PHILOSOPHER MORE, WHO SAID: "THE MORE ONE GIVES TO OTHERS, THE MORE HE HAS FOR HIS OWN. MEN WILL COME TO SEE MORE CLEARLY, NOT THAT WHICH DEVIDES THEM, BUT THAT WHICH UNITES THEM." tNSTEAD OF COMPETING AGAINST QTHERS FOR MATERIAL GAIN ALONE, THE GREATEST COMPETITION SHOULD TAKE PLACE WITHIN THE INDIVIDUAL. AS WE ENTER INTO THIS MOST CHRISTIAN MONTH OF THE YEAR, WE ARE BOUND TO FIND TIME FOR REFLECTION AND MEDITATION. EVERY NORMAL INDIVIDUAL, MAN OR WOMAN, IS MORE CONCERNED ABOUT THE FUTURE OF THOSE HE LEAVES BEHIND, THAN HE IS ABOUT HIMSELF. THIS NATURAL DESIRE TO LEAVE A BETTER WORLD HAS BEEN A STRONGER FORCE THAN IS GENERALLY RECOGNIZED. CHRISTMAS IS WHEN WE FEEL THIS STRONGLY. EXPERIENCE HAS TAUGHT US NOT TO EXPECT TOO MUCH. NO MATTER HOW HIGH OUR IDEALS ARE, WE CAN BE THANKFUL IF WE HAVE DONE OUR UTMOST TO BRING A GREATER UNDERSTANDING FOR THOSE SIMPLE BUT EVERLASTING PRINCIPLES THAT LED OUR FOLLOWERS TO A HIGHER MORAL LEVEL OF LIFE. AFTER CHRISTMAS COMES THE NEW YEAR, 1977- ALL OF US ARE LOOKING TOWARDS THE NEW YEAR WITH HOPES, DREAMS AND ALL THE BEST EXPECTATIONS. THE NEW YEAR WILL BRING US NEW RESPONSIBILITIES AND NEW CHALLENGES. LET'S APPROACH 1977 UNIFIED - UNIFIED WE CAN REACH HIGHER GOALS. LET THE TRUE SPIRIT OF THIS CHRISTMAS SEASON, IT'S JOY AND HOPE AND ABOVE ALL IT'S BINDING FAITH LIVE WITH US FOR MANY YEARS TO COME. A VERY MERRY CHRISTMAS AND A HAPPY AND PROSPEROUS NEW YEAR TO ALL OF YOU. NAZDAR DECEMBER 1976 CHRISTMAS COMING!
