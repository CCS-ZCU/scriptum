Bicentenial and American Sokol Ethnic groups across the nation will compete in demonstrating who made the greatest effort to build America. Before the American Sokol Organization was founded in the United States,a Czech religious group called "The Moravian Brothers", inspired by John Amn;? Comenius (Komenský), enriched the early educational system, as well as the spiritual life on this continent. Later, other Czech pioneers such as Augustin Herman, Vojta Náprstek, Frantisek Korbel, many farmers and skilled professional workers, made themselves a great asset in the development and growth of their new American country. These people founded their own ethnic groups. While, in Prague in 1862, Miroslav Tyrs and Jindřich Fugner founded the Sokol Organization and enriched the Czech nation with Sokol ideals, these Czech immigrants founded Sokol units across America giving the people an example of excellence in the area of gymnastics. It was not only the physical education that became important in their instruction, but also the respect of law and how to become a good American citizen. They built gymnasiums to organize a physical fitness program, and it is regrettable that other similar national organizations such as Sokol, were not spreading the same concept around America. When our countrymen and especially Sokol members volunteered to fight against nazism, they also fought for American democracy as well. Even at the present time, when our Czechoslovak people and especially Sokols take their stand against communism, they stand and preserve American democracy. What other ethnic group in America has helped to contribute in the building of this country in such a way? Help Our Sokol Grow [pageend1]Page 2.
