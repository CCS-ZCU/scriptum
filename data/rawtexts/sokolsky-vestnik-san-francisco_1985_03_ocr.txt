VI. SOKOL SLET Czechoslovak SOKOL Abroad June 1986 in Curich Sokol Slets are important gymnastic festivals of Sokol organizations. It is the result of their physical training and discipline. The program consists mostly of physical culture of Sokol youth as well as adults to show their fitness in all departments of physical education. The program is enriched by musical and artistic numbers. Also championship contests are held in which both individuals and groups of various units participate. The Americal Sokol Organizations have already held many Slets. The first Slet was in 1879 in New York. In free Czechoslovakia, Slets of Sokols, were held every six years in Prague. As an young man, he left his mother country in search of "Promised Land"- the United States of America. When he arrived he found the country in depression and has to work hard and many long hours in the struggle for his own survival.But he, like the others in that times - survived,Very soon he was enrolled in Sokol brotherhood and other national organizations. Tony was always there, doing what he could and not one to seek recognition, but to give his support in the background where little or no thanks ever extends. Brother Tony Prudek passed away February 8, 1985. For the Sokol members in San Francisco we extend our heartfelt sympathy to sister Marie Kohoutek and her family. To you br. Tony we express our gratitude for all you have done for our organization.All of us are and should be proud to have known you as good friend and brother.Your memory shall not be forgotten.
