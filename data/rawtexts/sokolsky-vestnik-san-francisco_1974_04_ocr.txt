We are privileged because we are members of Sokoli Our Sokol Unit, in the past two or three years has come a long way. We have broken records in participants at a National Slet. We’ have originated some new ideas in the way of raising money, we have seen more and more people attending events at our Hall here in San Mateo, and there are still things in the planning stage that could bring us more success. We are in the middle of plans for one of the biggest events our Unit has had in over 10 years, and we seem lax in putting forth the effort needed to make this a really successful week-end - not only financially but by spreading Sokol Brotherhood to our Units and to our Community. Success doesn’t come by passing the buck to someone else. This event and all other Sokol events would be far easier and much more successful if everyone would do something to help. We ARE Privileged and WE ARE pledged to up-hold the Sokol Ideals. Perhaps a review of our Sokol Ideals and the Sokol By-laws is needed for us to recall what being a Sokol member means. As to personal feelings, Br. Meier and I are not working to impress anyone! We dedicate and donate our time to Sokol because we truly believe in the Sokol Ideals - that the Tyrs System is a fine all around system for creating physically sound, morally strong, mentally alert young men and women who will soon be responsible for our Nation and our Sokol. Brotherhood. We are a bit discouraged! To close, let me read a poem recently published in a Vestnik from South Omaha. -See next column- WHAT KIND OF MEMBER Are you an active member — The kind that would be missed? Or are you just contented That your name is on the list? Do you attend the meetings And mingle with the flock? Or do you stay at home And criticize and knock? Do you take an active part To help the work along? Or are you satisfied to be Of those that just belong? Do you ever voluntarily Help at the guiding stick? Or leave the work to just a few And talk about the clique? Come out to meetings often And help with hands and heart. Don't be just a member: But take an active part! Think this over, member, You know right from wrong; Are you an active member, Or do you just belong?? Are you really doing your share? Think about it!
