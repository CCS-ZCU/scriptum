While we do not impugn the integrity of the convenors of the conference, we feel that we must comment on one of the aims of the convention. This aim is the recognition of the "People's Republic of China". Any recognition of Red China would, we feel, be a mistake. The first consequence of our recognition of Communist China which we need to consider would be the precedent set for recognizing other governments undemocratically constituted by force.
