Physical training, when practiced under qualified supervision, is a magnificent tool that serves a dual purpose. While it develops a strong and healthy body it unconsciously instills the characteristics of perseverence, thinking, patience and teamwork. The training that we give to the youth and our members is not intended to make champions or professionals out of a few, but is for the benefit of all so that they can become and remain physically fit. Therefore, we strive first and foremost, to meet the needs of the people as a whole, but we also recognize the right of the individual and provide every opportunity for each person to rise as high as their ability will take them. Sokol is not opposed to spectator sports, but come from "taking part" and not watching • Sokol strives to create in each member a continuing state of physical fitness. It strives toward mental, physical and moral fitness through a comprehensive course of physical education. Sokol aims at building "A sound mind in a sound body" through a wholesome program of physical education and through other social, cultural And educational activities.
