The first Issue of JARO appeared in the JEDNOTA o d April 15. 1921. For several years, however, the new venture of the Jednota Society did not meet with too much success. Most of the articles were written in Slovak by adults and for adults. There waa little Lhal appealed to those in their teens or young adulthood. On April 13, 1927. the format of JARO was changed.
