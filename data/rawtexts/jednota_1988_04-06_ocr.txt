Jan Vrabel (Jolly Jack Robel) of Shenandoah, Pa.: Jan "Jack" Vrabel was admitted to FCSU Junior Order. Branch 50. Shenandoah. Pa., at the age of six. He was transferred to the Senior Order, the St Stephen Society. Branch 114, at the age of 16. He was elected Recording Secretary of the Lodge, an office he held for four years.
