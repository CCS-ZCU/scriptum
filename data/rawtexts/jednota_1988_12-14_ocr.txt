HGod is all powerful and all good, why is there so much suffering and misery in the world? Why sb much sickness? Why doesn’t God protect us from disease and death? Why does God disappoint when we pray for the people we love? The rational mind seeks persuasive answers to these questions. We want to make God our servant: we try to fit Him into our plans and priorities. But God doesn’t fit. The only sign He seems lo give us year after year Is die sign of the cross. For 200D years the death of Christ has been reenacted on every parish altar throughout the world, And what does the cross Say Ln answer to all our educated questions? It says we should be alert to a thoroughly different way of thinking. Jesus died for values we tend to disparage. He took a stand that made Him a misfit and an alien in Bls own community. He had the divine perspective, avision which disturbed and challenged the people of His time and the people of all Limes. Jesus was making political statements in His Sermon on the Mount. He said that God's Kingdom Is filled with those who hunger and thirst for justice. He comforted the afflicted and afflicted the comfortable, and He kept It up even when powerful forces mounted against Him. He refused to bend and they killed Him without mercy. Most of us have inherited suburban, middle class values. We like comfort and a structured way of life. We respect success, cleanliness, and good manners.
