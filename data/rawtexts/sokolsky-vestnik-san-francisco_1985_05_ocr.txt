Last month many thousands students from High Schools and Colleges went through graduation ceremonies and they heard mostly the same speaches again, when their teachers and professors told them what wonderfull students they were, that this is the best generation this country ever had, most knowledgeable and the brightest etc. I do not denied that this may be the truth but in my mind is a question, who is responsible for all the credits the younger generation is collecting now? Nobody willl probably mention the middle age and old people sitting in audience and applauding their children and. grandchildren. Nobody will give them credit what they did and what achievements they reached.Nobody will mention their parents and grandparents, and they are the one who should be tha nked on this graduation day. I would like to reverse the process and tell the young people something like this: These are the people who have given you better and healthier world, than they found.And because of this you no longer have to fear epidemics of flu,typhus,diptheria, smallpox,rnesles,tuberculosis and others that they knew in their youth. Let me remind you that these remarkable people - your parents and grandparents - lived through history’s greatest depression.Many of these people know what is to be really poor, what is to be hungry and cold.And because of this experience,they determined it would not happen to you,that you would have better life,you would have food to eat, a warm house, better schools and graater opportunities to succeed than they had. Because they gave you the best, you are the tallest,helthiest and probably the best looking generation to inhabit the land.And because of them , you will work fewer hours, learn more, have more leisure time, travel to more and further places. These are also the people who fought man’s bloodiest wars,they are the people who defeated the tyrany of Hitler, and who when it was all over had the compassion to spend billions of dollars to help their former enemies rebuild their homelands. They build thousands of High Schools and Colleges, trained and hired ten of thousands of better teachers and at the same time made higher education available to milions of youngsters - where once it was only drean of few wealthy people. While the y have done all these things, they have had sone failures.They have not yet found the solution how to stop the wars. But they - these older generations - your parents and grandparents - made more progress than in any previous era in history, and don’t you ever forget it.If yourgs* neration can make as much progress in as many areas as these two generations have,you should be able to solve a many remaining ills of the world.But it won’t be easy. And you won't do it by tearing down or burningiU You may and can do it by hard work and positive thinking, with faith in this nation and in mankind. Jan Kučera Try it. Po třech letech se mu podařilo emigrovat do USA do města Bostonu. Nespokojil se však s podřadným zaměstnáním. Začal studovat americké právo a byl promován na amerického právníka.
